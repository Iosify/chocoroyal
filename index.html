Photography Direction
Lighting
Warm, directional natural light. Deep shadows. No harsh white studio light. Chiaroscuro-inspired depth.
Props & Surfaces
Dark marble, aged wood, linen napkins. Gold cutlery. No plastic props. No sterile white surfaces.
Colour Grade
Warm shadow tones. Lifted highlights. Rich, film-like contrast. Avoid oversaturation or cool-toned filters.
Composition
Rule of thirds. Negative space is a feature, not wasted space. Mix overhead flat lay with 45° hero shots.
