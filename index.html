Photography Direction
Lighting
Warm, directional natural light. Deep shadows. No harsh white studio light. Chiaroscuro-inspired depth - candle warmth over flash brightness.
Props & Surfaces
Dark marble, aged timber, linen napkins. Gold cutlery. Dusty rose ceramics where possible. No plastic props. No sterile white surfaces.
Colour Grade
Warm shadow tones. Lifted highlights. Rich, film-like contrast. Avoid oversaturation or cool filters. The image should feel like velvet.

Front Cover

  • Minimal Layout: Centered logo, large script brand name, spaced-out tagline.
  • Elements: "Dinner Menu", Small crest ♜.
  • Design Rule: 60% empty space. No prices. No clutter.
Panel 2

Inside Left

  • Sections: Salads, Starters.
  • Layout: Two stacked sections on soft pink background panels.

Inside Right

  • Sections: Main Courses, Sides, Desserts.
